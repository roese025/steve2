Steve Prefontaine

Exhibit

1 One Bowerman Dr, Beaverton, OR

July 10th - July 17th

Join us to celebrate and honor Steve Prefontaine.
Steve is one of the biggest figures in Track and Field
and was known for up front racing strategy.
His track career at the University of Oregon will
always have an impact on track and field.
The exhibit is held at the Nike Headquarters
from 10 am to 4 pm.
